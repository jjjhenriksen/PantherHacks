2025 Panther Hacks HealthCare track project!

By Jacqueline Henriksen, Melissa Campos Huallanca, and San San Goh

Feeling unwell or a little low?

Sometimes, getting to a doctor right away isn’t easy — but that’s where the Wellness Wizard steps in. With a sprinkle of digital magic and a touch of care, he’s here to help you make sense of your symptoms and point you in the right direction.

Whether you’re battling a bothersome sniffle, a curious cough, or just a mysterious sense of “meh,” the Wellness Wizard is here to lend a sparkly hand (or wand!).

Simply type what you’re experiencing into this chat box — whether it’s a strange ache, or just that “something’s not right” feeling — and the Wellness Wizard will start working his spellbook of medical knowledge. He’ll ask you a few quick questions to better understand what’s going on, then offer useful insights, self-care tips, and gentle guidance.

So, if you’re feeling a bit under the weather or just need a little reassurance, don’t hesitate to reach out. The Wellness Wizard is here to help you feel better, one chat at a time.
