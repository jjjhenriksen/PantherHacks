Resurrection of the Stars

A 15+, literate text-based Warriors RP on Discord.

There were no saints. There were no sinners. Only chilling neutrality. A void, where all the dead go to rest, with nothing but cold, dark, depth. The Stars had not been created by those who held belief in them. These four Clans are struggling with the rise of new rogue groups in their midst, with an old guard of spirits re-emerging after centuries of dormancy. . .
